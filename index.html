DECENTRALIZED

Electrum is free software released under the MIT License.

Anyone can run an Electrum server.

VERIFIABLE

Electrum verifies that your transactions are in the Bitcoin blockchain. Because Bitcoin is not about trust. It is about freedom and independence.

Electrum Wallet verifies all the transactions in your history using SPV.
